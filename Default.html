<h2>About HIUF</h2>
<p class="lead">Highly Innovative Unique Foundation (HIUF) is a Saudi foundation concerned with the exploration of inventors and innovators in the different technical and scientific fields. It also facilitates the participations and creative works in the international inventions exhibitions all over the world. It works to marketing the innovations and inventions by promoting them into the media, in the different social media and to the businessmen and investors for their works manufacturing. HIUF will provide the appropriate solutions to all difficulties that may face by some inventors and finally it will provide the scientific consultations according to the need of the inventors.</p>
<h2>Vision</h2>
<p class="lead">Excellent Community Service with High Credibility and Marked Distinction.</p>
<h2>Mission</h2>
<p class="lead">Develop the creative ideas and transform them into a viable practical product to be presenting in the various international exhibitions and getting high excellent reputation which will encourage that will be transformed to an implemented ideas in our life.</p>
</div>
<h3>Our Services</h3>
<p>
* Provide consulting support for the inventors to improve their inventions.
<br />
* Present the inventions on behalf of inventors in the different international inventions exhibitions.
<br />
* Document all the international participations events and provide them with up to date information.
<br />
* Explain all inventions for countries committee participating in the various international exhibitions in order to obtain special prizes.
<br />
* Present the inventions to the investors and businessmen when they visit exhibitions for the purpose of obtaining support to turn them into applied products.
<br />
* Provide adequate media coverage of the foundation’s participation in the international exhibition according to its excellent results.
<br />
* Provide specialized training courses in creative thinking and hoe to obtain prizes.
<br />
* Issuing a bi-annual journal about foundation activities.
<br />
* There are more and more under study.
</p>
